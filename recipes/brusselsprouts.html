Roasted Brussel Sprouts

Directions

These brussel sprouts come out crispy and flavorful despite having little seasoning. They also cook in 25 minutes!

Ingredients

  • 1 lb Brussel Sprouts
  • 2 Tbsp Olive Oil
  • Salt and Pepper to taste

Directions

  1. Preheat the oven to 425 degrees.
  2. Wash and dry brussel sprouts.
  3. Cut sprouts in half vertically. Discard any brown leaves that may come loose.
  4. In a medium bowl, combine sprouts, oil, salt, and pepper. Toss to coat.
  5. Line a baking sheet with parchment paper.
  6. Place sprouts cut side down on the baking sheet. Bake for 25 minutes.
