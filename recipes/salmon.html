Baked Salmon

Description

Salmon is an easy to prepare dish and requires only a few simple ingredients. Plus, it's incredibly healthy!

Ingredients

  • 1 lb Salmon
  • Salt
  • Pepper
  • Lemon
  • Cooking Spray

Directions

  1. Preheat the oven to 425 degrees.
  2. Cut salmon into 4 4oz portions.
  3. Sprinkle the salmon with salt and pepper to taste.
  4. Line a baking dish with aluminum foil, then spray with foil with cooking spray.
  5. Place the salmon onto the baking sheet and cook for 12 minutes, or until salmon flakes easily with a fork.
  6. Squeeze lemon juice over each piece of salmon and serve.
