Roasted Butternut Squash

Description

This butternut squash comes out soft and tender. Pairs well with Oven Baked Salmon.

Ingredients

  • 1 medium Butternut Squash
  • 2 Tbsp Olive Oil
  • 1 Tbsp Italian Seasoning
  • Salt and Pepper to taste

Directions

  1. Preheat oven to 425 degrees.
  2. Using a vegetable peeler, peel the squash and cut in half vertically using a chef's knife (be very careful!).
  3. Scoop out the seeds from the squash. Discard the seeds.
  4. Cut the squash into 1/2 inch cubes.
  5. In a medium bowl, add cubed squash, oil, and seasonings. Toss to combine.
  6. Spread squash onto an aluminum baking sheet and bake for 30 minutes.
